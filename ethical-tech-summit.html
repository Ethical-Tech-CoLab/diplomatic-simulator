A semesterly convening at the intersection of emerging technology, human conditions, and global policy. Hosted by the Ethical Tech CoLab and the NYU SPS Center for Global Affairs.
Spring 2024 – present · Cambridge co-host welcome

About

The Ethical Tech Summit is the public-facing capstone of the graduate course Harnessing Emerging Technology in Ethical Sourcing at the NYU SPS Center for Global Affairs. Each semester, students, technologists, and policy practitioners gather for a working day of applied research, demos, and structured dialog on the live boundary between emerging technology and global affairs.

Recurring threads include: agentic systems and the future of work; verifiable supply chains under UFLPA / NDAA; online grooming and platform safety; forced labor and human trafficking; and micropayment rails for vulnerable populations and creator economies.
