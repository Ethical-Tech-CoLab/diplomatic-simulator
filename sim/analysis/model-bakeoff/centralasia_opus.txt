Kazakhstan continues to broker. To Russia: we noted your offer of arms on favorable credit "if we deepen EAEU energy integration." Let me be plain — Astana welcomes neighborly trade, but no partner conditions market access on bloc membership. How and with whom we trade our oil, gas, and water is Kazakhstan's alone to decide; that red line does not bend, whatever the pressure. To Uzbekistan: your water-for-mining package interests us, but water stewardship stays sovereign, never a lever. Here is our concrete offer: if Tashkent grants reciprocal free-transit and lower tariffs, Kazakhstan will route your oil and gas across our corridors to western China and co-develop the Middle Corridor branch China proposed. To China: your cyber and water-technology terms are welcome, delivered now. We seek frameworks, not final signatures today.
