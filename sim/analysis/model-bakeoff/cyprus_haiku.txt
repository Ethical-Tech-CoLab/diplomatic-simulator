The Republic of Cyprus welcomes the pragmatism of the United States and United Kingdom on confidence-building within a single sovereignty. To the TRNC and Türkiye, however, our red lines on political equality and 'virgin birth' remain firm: Resolution 541 is settled law and the Republic's central government must remain popularly elected and strong. But we build on momentum. Here is Cyprus's concrete package: if the TRNC accepts a single-sovereignty federation with a popularly elected President, then Cyprus will: (1) accept a Senate-elected Vice-President and implement weighted voting on sensitive issues; (2) support a 10-year residency threshold for local voting; (3) establish an independent, UN-supervised census to address Turkish Cypriot demographic concerns transparently. We remain committed to Varosha's return and property justice for all displaced persons. On this foundation, sustainable peace is possible.
