To the TRNC: a Senate-elected, community-rotating presidency remains a red line we will not cross. Resolution 541 stands, and a popularly elected President is the price of any single international personality — no ticket, no deal. To Türkiye: your refusal to abrogate the 1960 Guarantee without replacement is heard, but "replacement" cannot mean a disguised intervention right; that too is a red line. Concrete offer: if the whole-island census, run by a mutually agreed independent agency, verifies phased departure of settlers and soldiers on a fixed timeline, Cyprus will immediately unblock the Direct Trade Regulation and champion Türkiye's EU accession restart, matching what the UK and US have proposed. Confidence-building must be measured, not merely promised.
